Quick Bio

I was born March 4, 2001 in Minnesota. I am the 7th of 10 kids in my family. There are 7 boys and 3 girls. Because of that, there was always things going on at my house. Despite there not being much privacy or quietness, I love being part of a large family.

Here are a few fun facts about me from my high school years. I went to Mounds View High School where I participated in several activities. I was the Captain of my school's Bowling Team and Men's Volleyball Team. Senior year, I was part of the Ball Squad (my schools table tennis team), and we placed 3rd in the state tournament. Despite being tall and skinny, I was on my school's wrestling team for 3 years. I also enjoyed being a drummer in my school's jazz band.

I recently completed my Freshman year at the University of Nebraksa - Lincoln. I am part of a really cool honors program there called the Raikes School for Computer Science and Management. I am majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Business Management. I really enjoyed going to the sporting events, especially Volleyball because Nebraska has a really great team. I am also involved in the Newman Center at college.
